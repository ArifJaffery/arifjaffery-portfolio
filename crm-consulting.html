Ref / 05 — Commercial models

Two ways to fund delivery — built for how you budget

Every engagement can be structured as a turnkey solution — discovery through go-live and hypercare — priced under either model below.

Model A
Fixed Cost
Scope locked. Price locked.
  • Deliverables, timeline and price agreed up front against a defined scope
  • Milestone-based payments tied to working software, not hours logged
  • Best for well-defined module rollouts with stable requirements
  • Predictable budgeting — no surprise invoices at month end
Model B
Cost-Plus on License
Delivery effort + license, fully transparent.
  • Time & materials for delivery effort, billed transparently
  • Platform licence cost passed through at an agreed, disclosed margin
  • Best for phased rollouts or scope that evolves as you learn
  • Full visibility into what's delivery fee and what's licence cost

Turnkey solutions across every module.From discovery workshops and solution design through build, data migration, integration, go-live and ongoing hypercare support — delivered by one accountable consultant.
