The framework

A retrieval pipeline built to answer, not just search

We ingest your real documents, index them for meaning rather than keywords, and orchestrate retrieval and response generation as durable, observable steps — so every answer is grounded in what you actually publish.

Client-facing question and answer Retrieval and generation calls

01

Ingest & structure

Product sheets, compliance reports, investor decks, and case studies are chunked and prepared for retrieval.

02

Embed & index

Each chunk is embedded and stored in a vector database you control, so answers stay grounded in your content.

03

Orchestrate

Inngest runs retrieval and generation as separate, durable steps — each one retried and logged independently.

04

Retrieve & generate

OpenAI drafts the answer strictly from the retrieved context, with the source passages carried alongside it.

05

Deliver

The answer reaches your client through a chat widget, your sales tools, or a simple API call — wherever they already are.

Why it matters: because retrieval and generation run as independent, durable steps, a slow response from one service never loses the work already done by another — and every run is traceable end to end.
